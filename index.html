Regulations

Rules & Eligibility

Eligibility

  • Must play on the NA/SA server.
  • Open to all players regardless of guild affiliation.
  • One player, one team — no switching after draft.
  • Must be available Mon & Thu 8:30–10PM EST.

Class Restrictions

  • Only one restriction applies: Cordy Xbow ×2 max per team at any time.
  • All other classes and builds are completely unrestricted.
  • Bring whatever composition your team decides.

Match Conduct

  • Respectful behaviour required at all times.
  • No exploits, bugs, or third-party tools permitted.
  • Scoreboard screenshots must be submitted after each match.
  • Admin team has final say on all disputes.
  • Harassment or abuse results in immediate removal.

Draft Rules

  • Draft conducted live via stream on April 30th EST.
  • Co-captains selected by captains before the stream.
  • Picks are blind — captains cannot see each other's selections.
  • Roster: 48 players + 18 subs (may scale to 54).
